A Note from the Developer

Hello user,


I am Russell, the developer behind this personal virtual assistant application. I wanted to personally greet you and thank you for using my application. What started out as an idea I had on a business trip train ride came into fruition after hours spent researching the possibility of integrating different services/products of different companies within a framework that I was previously unfamiliar with. I have always been interested in the idea of having a customizable personal assistant, one who is tailored to the user's preferences while being able to conversate in an all natural manner, be it for learning, browsing or just as a companion.

Given the aspects of unfamiliarity in engineering this application, I was initially hesitant at first; considering the prospect of having to juggle school work and an internship (at the time of building this project) simultaneously. However, someone once told me that taking the time to invest in yourself is worth far more in the long run. I hope that one day, as I look back on this project I will be reminded of this statement and remember what it takes to be an engineer.
